Welcome to our experiment.

This is the digit span test. 
The test is consist of two parts.
the first one is the forward digit span test. The other one is the backward digit span test.

In the forward digit span test, you will listen to a sequence of numbers and have to type them in the exact order you heard the numbers. 
In the backward version you have to type the sequence in a reversed order. 

Please put on the head phones for this test.


Press SPACE to start.
